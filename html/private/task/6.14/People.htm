Distinctive Capabilities

 

Strategies

The OCOB is a diverse community of caring and spirited people who, through their practice of honest communication and commitment to learning, create a welcoming and engaging educational environment.  

 

Satisfaction of various OCOB constituents

·         Create forums for exploration of staff, faculty, lecturers, student, and alumni satisfaction with OCOB programs, goals, policies, procedures and the college environment.

·         Use survey research methods to measure staff, faculty, lecturer, student and alumni satisfaction with OCOB programs, goals, policies, procedures, and environment.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Shared expectations and communications

·         Establish orientation processes for staff, faculty, and lecturers that include OCOB & Cal Poly mission, policies & procedures. 

·         Identify and implement best mentoring practices to facilitate the FAR, RPT and staff evaluation processes

·         Encourage dialogue within the OCOB regarding shared values and performance expectations.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Communication through celebrations of accomplishments

·         Identify a broader list of topics (for example, service to the community, relationships with employers, and advisement) that are deserving of recognition, and find more ways to celebrate noteworthy performance that supports the OCOB mission

·         Develop processes for sharing a diverse range of accomplishments

 

 

Measures

 

·         Satisfaction of various OCOB constituents

·         Shared expectations and communications

·         Communication through celebrations of accomplishments

·         Quality and cultural diversity

·         Sense of community

 

 

 

 

Quality and cultural diversity

·         Develop procedures for recruiting and retaining quality staff, faculty, lecturers, and students.

·         Develop activities and dedicate resources to the study and visible celebration of cultural diversity among staff, faculty, lecturers, and students.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Sense of community

§         Establish social and community-building events that will foster a climate of collegiality and sense of belonging within the OCOB. (Events should promote the removal of the barriers among staff/faculty/lecturers/administration)

§         Develop a culture of participation within the OCOB

§         Encourage cross disciplinary endeavors
