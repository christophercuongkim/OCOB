MBA Program Learning Goals and Objectives (Final GPC adopted version AY 2010-11)

Mission Statement:  "We are an engaged learning community (LG1) that contributes to business (LG4/LG5) and society (LG2/LG3) through discovery and application (LG1)."

Learning Goals (LG):  Students graduating from our program shall be…

Learning Objectives (LO):  Students graduating from our program will be able to…

LG 1

Competent in business acumen.

LO 1.1

Demonstrate competency in the following areas of business: management, quantitative methods, economics, accounting, finance, marketing, operations and strategy.

LO 1.2

Demonstrate strategic integration of the above areas.

LO 1.3

Demonstrate the ability to apply analytics to decision making.

LG 2

Aware of business ethics.

LO 2.1

Recognize issues and solutions using an approach that reflects ethical values.

LG 3

Capable of valuing a global perspective.

LO 3.1

Demonstrate knowledge of the issues involved in conducting business in a diverse, global environment.

LG 4

Effective communicators.

LO 4.1

Demonstrate professional written communication.

LO 4.2

Demonstrate professional oral communication and presentation skills.

LG 5

Effective leaders.

LO 5.1

Recognize leadership skills and link to leadership theory.

LO 5.2

Demonstrate effective team behaviors.
