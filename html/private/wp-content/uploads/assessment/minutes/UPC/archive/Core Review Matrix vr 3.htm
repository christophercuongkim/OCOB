Mission Statement      
The Orfalea College of Business challenges highly motivated students to become tomorrow's socially responsible business leaders through a learn-by-doing, technology-oriented education      
Skills and Competencies      
A.  Intellectual      
..Be skilled at appropriate applications of theoretical concepts
..Capabilities for inquiry, abstract logical thinking, inductive and deductive reasoning and critical analysis
..Ability to exercise judgment by identifying and solving unstructured problems in unfamiliar settings  
B.  Interpersonal, Team and Leadership      
..Ability to work with others, particularly in groups, to influence them, to lead them, to organize and delegate tasks, to motivate and develop people, and to withstand and resolve conflict.
..Ability to interact with culturally diverse people.
C.  Communication      
..Ability to present, discuss, and defend views effectively through formal and informal written and spoken language
..Ability to listen effectively
D.  Cross Functional Thinking      
..The ability to apply knowledge from various functional disciplines and to perceive how that knowledge must be integrated in a cross-disciplinary and holistic fashion to reach managerial solutions.  
E.  Values, Ethics.      
..Recognition of the importance of developing a personal value structure and an understanding and appreciation for organizational values which they might encounter.
..The ability to identify ethical issues and apply a value based reasoning system to ethical questions.
F. Technical and Practical      
..Recognition of current and emerging technologies and their impact on organization and society
..Knowledge of latest information technology tools
..Ability to locate, obtain, organize, report, and use information from human, print, and electronic sources (both domestic and international)
G.  Business Context      
..Awareness of global developments and impact on industries and organizations
..Awareness of external forces that impact business
UPC Guiding Principles      
OCOB graduates will understand the fundamentals of how a successful enterprise operates.  
Graduates will have sufficient depth in an area of study to begin a successful career in an organization by providing immediate value.
